The problem

Most brand problems are structural, not visual

They are hard to detect in internal reviews and design discussions.

Common symptoms:

  • People don't clearly understand what your product does
  • Messaging changes across pages
  • Positioning becomes vague over time
  • Teams focus on design instead of clarity

By the time these issues become obvious, they already affect growth.

You can't fix what you can't see

The solution

Xraylo reveals your brand structure

Xraylo analyzes your brand like a diagnostic system. Instead of subjective opinions, it evaluates structural elements:

  • Positioning clarity
  • Messaging consistency
  • Value proposition strength
  • Brand differentiation
  • Communication structure

The result is a clear report showing where your brand works — and where it needs improvement.
